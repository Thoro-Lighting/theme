Module tasks

The module has two main tasks:
1. Blocking other modules before sending transactions to Google Analytics. This prevents inter alia duplication of transactions.
2. Sending the transaction to Google Analytics, the statuses of orders defined in the configuration module.

Manual sending orders from the store to Google Analytics

By accessing the sub module 'Send transactions to GTA', you can send the selected order from the store to Google Analytics.

Just type identifiers orders or specify their ranges, such as:
1,2,4,7 - orders to send: 1,2,4,7
1,2,4-7 - orders to send: 1,2,4,5,6,7
1,2,9-7 - orders to send: 1,2,7,8,9

Warnings

It should be so set the status of orders that they were not sent several times to the GA.

Eg. Setting 'payment accepted', then 'order canceled' and finally again 'payment accepted' - will make the order twice will go to Google Analytics.
Assuming that the transactions are sent to Google Analytics the status of 'payment accepted' - depends on the module configuration.
